Question Writing

In the summer of 2024, I and a couple other seniors from Kentucky who fancied playing "Academic Team" (Kentucky's version of quizbowl/scholarbowl) started the first iteration of our own Academic Team-esque question set known as SKATABowl. In the summer of 2025, we began working on the second edition, coined 2KATABowl. We're out of luck for the third edition name, but surely the potential fourth and fifth set will be fine. You can find a link to them below: (I'll do this later when I build the SKATA writing team website!)
